Julius Yego

The 'YouTube Man'

Julius Yego at the 2015 World Championships Final.

Julius Yego is a Kenyan javelin thrower. Born in January 4th 1989 in Tinderet,Nandi District in Rift Valley,Kenya. His story is one of dedication, focus and dilligence and is a source of inspiration for aspiring athletes in his home country and worldwide.

Coming from a humble background,he is famed for using YouTube as a training resource to improve his technique and fitness levels,watching idols like Czech record holder,Jan Zelezny.

"My personal inner desire and will to achieve the best I could drove me"

Julius Yego

These are his career highlights:

  • 2006- won national junior title breaking Kenyan junior record
  • 2008,2009,2010-Wins Kenyan national javelin three times in a row.
  • 2010-Wins a bronze medal in the African Championships in Athletics,held in Nairobi with a personal best of 75.44m.
  • 2013-Becomes first Kenyan to qualify for a field event final in the World Championship and Earns fourth place in the World Championships held in Moscow with a 85.4m throw.
  • 2014- Becomes first Kenyan to win a Commonwealth gold medal in a field event with a throw of 83.87m,and went ahead to clinch a gold at the African Championship that same year with a distance of 84.72m
  • 2015-His star continues to rise as he wins the Ostrava Golden Spike javelin on May 26 with a new Kenyan record of 86.88m going on to this with a distance of 87.71m at the Golden Gala in Rome with a second place finish to Vitezslav Vesely. Three days later,Yego won the Grand Prix in Birmingham beating Vesely with an impressive 91.39m throw, setting a new Diamond League and Africa record.
  • 2016- He clinched a silver medal at the Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro.
